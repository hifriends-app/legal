6. Deletion Rights

Cascade Delete

When you delete a photo of yourself:

  • It's removed from ALL users on HiFriends within 48 hours
  • This includes: your feed, photographer's camera roll, all other recipients' feeds
  • Also removed from: your profile page and anyone else's profile page where it was published
  • It's permanent and can't be undone
  • The photographer is NOT notified
  • The photographer may notice it's gone from their camera roll

Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • All your data is deleted
  • All your biometric data (face encodings) is permanently destroyed within 30 days
  • Photos you took remain with recipients (without your name)
  • Process completes within 30 days
