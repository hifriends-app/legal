HiFriends Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

Quick Summary

What HiFriends Does:

  • Uses face recognition to automatically deliver photos to people in them
  • Lets you delete any photo of yourself from everywhere on HiFriends
  • Never sells your data

What We Need:

  • Your face data to recognize you in photos
  • Basic account info (name, email, username)
  • You must be 13+ to use HiFriends

Your Rights:

  • Delete any photo of yourself anytime
  • Delete your entire account anytime
  • Request a copy of all your data

1. Introduction

Welcome to HiFriends! This Privacy Policy explains how HiFriends Inc. ("we," "us") handles your information when you use our app.

By using HiFriends, you agree to this policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the app.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email, username, password

Face Recognition Data

How it works:

  • During setup, we capture photos of your face
  • We convert these into mathematical data (face encodings)
  • We only keep the encodings to recognize you
  • Original face photos captured during enrollment are not retained after encoding extraction

Illinois residents: If you live in Illinois, you have special rights under BIPA law. We need your consent to collect face data, we'll never sell it, and you can delete it anytime.

Face Recognition Technology

Accuracy Notice:

  • Face recognition is not 100% accurate
  • Sometimes photos may be delivered to wrong people (false positives)
  • Sometimes photos may not be delivered to right people (false negatives)
  • Accuracy improves as you use the app

Photos

  • Photos you take
  • Photos you receive
  • Photos you publish to your profile
  • You can save photos you're in to your device
  • HiFriends does not prevent or notify you of screenshots. Photos shared on any platform can be captured this way. Our cascade delete removes photos from HiFriends but cannot affect screenshots already taken.

Basic Usage Info

  • How you use the app
  • Performance data
  • Device type and app version

3. How We Use Your Information

Core Features

  • Recognize you in photos and deliver them to you
  • Let you delete photos of yourself from everywhere
  • Manage your account and keep it secure

Improvements

  • Make face recognition more accurate
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Understand how people use HiFriends

Communication

  • Push notifications about new photos delivered to you (you can disable these in your settings)
  • Email you about account issues
  • Update you on important changes

Business Changes

If there are significant changes to HiFriends' business structure or ownership, we'll notify you before your data is affected by any different privacy practices.

5. Your Control & Rights

Delete Any Photo of Yourself

Cascade Delete: Remove a photo from everywhere on HiFriends:

  • Your feed
  • The photographer's camera roll
  • Everyone else who received it
  • Your profile page
  • Anyone else's profile page where it was published

How to delete: From your Me Feed, select the photo and confirm deletion. Deletion propagates across the platform within 48 hours. No notifications are sent to the photographer.

Delete Your Account

Deletes everything:

  • All your face data
  • All your photos
  • Your account info

"Not Me" Feedback

If you get a photo that's not you, mark it "Not Me" to:

  • Remove it from your Me Feed
  • Help improve recognition accuracy

Request Your Data

To request a copy of your data, email support@hifriends.ai with subject "Data Request" from your registered email address. We'll respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Limited access controls

Important: No system is 100% secure. If there's a breach, we'll notify you as required by law.

7. Data Retention

  • Face data: Kept while your account is active. Face encodings are permanently destroyed within 30 days of account deletion or when you exercise your deletion rights.
  • Photos: Kept until you delete them
  • Account info: Kept while your account is active
  • Deleted data: Removed from our systems within 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

You must be 13 or older to use HiFriends. By creating an account, you represent and warrant that you are at least 13 years old. If we learn someone under 13 created an account, we'll delete it immediately along with all associated data including biometric information.

Parents: Email us at support@hifriends.ai if your child under 13 has an account.

9. Location Information

We don't currently collect location data. In the future, we may add optional location features to improve your experience. If we do, we'll:

  • Update this policy
  • Ask your permission first
  • Make it completely optional
  • Let you control location sharing in your settings
