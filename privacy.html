HiFriends Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

Quick Summary

What HiFriends Does:

  • Uses face recognition to automatically deliver photos to people in them
  • Lets you delete any photo of yourself from everywhere on HiFriends
  • Never sells your data

What We Need:

  • Your face data to recognize you in photos
  • Basic account info (name, email, username)
  • You must be 13+ to use HiFriends

Your Rights:

  • Delete any photo of yourself anytime
  • Delete your entire account anytime
  • Request all your data

1. Introduction

Welcome to HiFriends! This Privacy Policy explains how HiFriends Inc. ("we," "us") handles your information when you use our app.

By using HiFriends, you agree to this policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the app.

Beta Notice: HiFriends is currently in beta testing. Features may change as we improve the service.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email, username, password

Face Recognition Data

How it works:

  • During setup, we capture photos of your face
  • We convert these into mathematical data (face encodings)
  • We only keep the encodings to recognize you

Illinois residents: If you live in Illinois, you have special rights under BIPA law. We need your consent to collect face data, we'll never sell it, and you can delete it anytime.

Face Recognition Technology

Accuracy Notice:

  • Face recognition is not 100% accurate
  • Sometimes photos may be delivered to wrong people (false positives)
  • Sometimes photos may not be delivered to right people (false negatives)
  • We use third-party technology providers for face recognition
  • Accuracy improves as you use the app

Photos

  • Photos you take
  • Photos you receive
  • Photos you publish to your profile
  • You can save photos you're in to your device
  • Anyone can screenshot without notification

Basic Usage Info

  • How you use the app
  • Performance data
  • Device type and app version

5. Your Control & Rights

Delete Any Photo of Yourself

Cascade Delete: Remove a photo from everywhere on HiFriends:

  • Your feed
  • The photographer's camera roll
  • Everyone else who received it
  • Your profile page
  • Anyone else's profile page where it was published

This deletion occurs within 48 hours. No notifications are sent to the photographer.

Delete Your Account

Deletes everything:

  • All your face data
  • All your photos
  • Your account info

"Not Me" Feedback

If you get a photo that's not you, mark it "Not Me" to:

  • Remove it from your Me Feed
  • Help improve recognition accuracy

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Limited access controls

Important: No system is 100% secure. If there's a breach, we'll notify you as required by law.

7. Data Retention

  • Face data: Kept while your account is active
  • Photos: Kept until you delete them
  • Account info: Kept while your account is active
  • Deleted data: Removed from our systems within 30 days
